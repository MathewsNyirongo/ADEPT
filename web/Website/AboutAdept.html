Company Overview

Adept Furnitures and Interiors is a professionally managed entity which aims at changing how Zambians view locally made products. It is in pursuit of this ultimate goal that makes us value our clients and ensure customer satisfactions at all costs. We are an indigenous Zambian company, which has provided employment to a number of people and intend to continue doing so, even as we grows from strength to strength.

General Business Activities

Adept Furnitures and Interiors has been established as a company that deals in wood related products. Our traditional business model is based on manufacturing a wide range of furniture and related products in Zambia. The company also does however, import products from other parts of the world for a wider selection to our esteemed clients. Our qualified staff, which comprises of highly trained locals and expatriates, has given an edge over our competitors
